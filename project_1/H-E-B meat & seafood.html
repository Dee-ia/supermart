Preparation Instructions

Heat charcoal 30 minutes or gas grill 10 minutes with lid closed grill 4" above Medium heat Hold palm just above grate. If heat causes you to pull away in 4 seconds heat is ideal (375 degrees F) for 3/4'' steak, cook 7-9 min/side for 1" steak, cook 10-12 min/side for Medium-Rare or Medium remove when 140 or 150 degree F internal temp
