Product Description

This is a great bbq pack to feed a crowd with, with loads of options.

Included in your pack is 500g or 1kg of:

  • lean beef mince
  • rump steak
  • lamb midloin chops
  • boneless and skinless chicken breasts
  • butterflied pork steaks
  • rindless middle bacon.

Make your own beef burger patties, marinate the rump steak and chicken, serve the BBQ lamb midloin chops with some good old kiwi mint sauce, and the pork steaks with your favourite salsa. And the bacon is there for breakfast in the morning!
